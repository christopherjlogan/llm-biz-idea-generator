What questions should you ask to generate a complete business startup plan for automating job descriptions with Generative AI?
Ask questions about the customer pain points, how Generative AI improves the process, and the potential market competition.
Use the answers to these questions to generate a business plan with the following structure: Business Problem, Solution Concept, Value Proposition, Plan of Action.
For the plan of action, explain the key milestones in product development and market entry.
After generating the startup plan, reflect on whether all critical aspects of the business idea were addressed.
